AMNI College of Nursing

Admissions Open for 2025

AMNI College of Nursing is dedicated to providing high-quality nursing education and training. Located in a vibrant academic setting, the college offers a robust curriculum that combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on clinical experience, preparing students for successful careers in healthcare. Our experienced faculty are committed to mentoring and supporting students throughout their educational journey. Equipped with modern facilities and a focus on community engagement, AMNI College of Nursing empowers students to excel in nursing practice and contribute positively to the health and well-being of individuals and communities.

AMNI College of Pharmacy

Programs Offered

AMNI College of Pharmacy offers comprehensive pharmaceutical education programs:

  • Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm-D) - 5 Years
  • Pharmacy Technician (Category B) - 2 Years
  • Diploma in Pharmacy - 2 Years
  • Certificate in Pharmacy - 1 Year
  • Continuing Education Programs

Admission Requirements

Eligibility criteria for pharmacy programs:

  • F.Sc. Pre-Medical (60% marks minimum)
  • Age: 17-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • English proficiency test
  • Pharmacy aptitude test

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work in various pharmaceutical settings:

  • Clinical Pharmacist
  • Hospital Pharmacist
  • Pharmaceutical Researcher
  • Drug Safety Specialist
  • Pharmacy Manager

AMNI College of Nursing

Programs Offered

AMNI College of Nursing provides comprehensive nursing education:

  • BS Nursing - 4 Years
  • Diploma in Nursing - 3 Years
  • Post RN BSN - 2 Years
  • Specialized Nursing Programs
  • Continuing Education in Nursing

Admission Requirements

Eligibility criteria for nursing programs:

  • F.Sc. Pre-Medical (60% marks minimum)
  • Age: 17-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • English proficiency test
  • Nursing aptitude test

Career Opportunities

Nursing graduates have diverse career options:

  • Registered Nurse (RN)
  • Clinical Nurse Specialist
  • Nurse Educator
  • Public Health Nurse
  • Nurse Administrator

AMNI College of Allied Health Sciences

Programs Offered

AMNI College of Allied Health Sciences offers various healthcare programs:

  • BS Medical Lab Technology - 4 Years
  • BS Anesthesia - 4 Years
  • Diploma in LHV - 2 Years
  • Diploma in CMW - 2 Years
  • Radiology Technology - 2 Years

Admission Requirements

Eligibility criteria for allied health programs:

  • F.Sc. Pre-Medical (60% marks minimum)
  • Age: 17-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • English proficiency test
  • Program-specific aptitude test

Career Opportunities

Allied health graduates have excellent career prospects:

  • Medical Lab Technologist
  • Anesthesia Technologist
  • Lady Health Visitor
  • Community Midwife
  • Radiology Technologist
