Abbottabad Campus

Our Abbottabad campus is a state-of-the-art facility dedicated to excellence in medical and nursing education. Located in the beautiful city of Abbottabad, our campus provides an ideal learning environment with modern facilities and experienced faculty.

The campus features advanced laboratories, well-equipped classrooms, a modern library, and clinical training facilities. Our students benefit from hands-on experience and practical training in healthcare settings.
