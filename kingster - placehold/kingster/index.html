About Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute

Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute is a leading institution dedicated to excellence in medical and nursing education. We empower future healthcare professionals with knowledge, skills, and values to serve communities with compassion and expertise.

Founded to advance healthcare education, Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute offers accredited programs in medicine and nursing. Our commitment to quality teaching, research, and community service prepares students to become leaders in the healthcare sector.
