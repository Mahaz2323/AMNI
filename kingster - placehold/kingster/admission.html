Detail About How To Apply

If you are passionate about making a real difference in people’s lives and want to study at a medical and nursing institute located in the heart of the city, then Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute is the right choice for you. At our institute, we don’t rely on rigid formulas to select students. Instead, we carefully review each applicant’s academic achievements and personal qualities to build a diverse, compassionate, and capable student community. We welcome individuals from all walks of life who are dedicated to healthcare, service, and creating a better tomorrow. Join us and become part of a dynamic environment that blends academic excellence with real-world medical training preparing future nurses, paramedics, and healthcare professionals to serve with skill, empathy, and integrity.

The Application Process

1

Start Online Submission

Begin your journey by accessing our online application portal. Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute makes it easy for aspiring students to take the first step toward a rewarding healthcare career — from the comfort of their homes.

2

Submit The Form

Carefully fill out the admission form with your academic and personal information. Ensure all required fields are completed honestly, as this is the foundation of your medical and nursing education with us.

3

Review The Submission

Before finalizing, review your entire application thoroughly. Check for accuracy, verify your academic records, and ensure your goals align with the values of Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute.

4

Gather Necessary Documents

Collect and prepare all supporting documents, such as transcripts, identity verification, recent photographs, and any certifications. Uploading the correct documents ensures a smooth evaluation process.

5

Interviewing Process

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview. This is an opportunity to express your motivation, passion for healthcare, and how you can contribute to the community through your role in the medical field.

6

Last Decision

Once all steps are complete, our admissions committee will carefully evaluate your profile. Final decisions are based on academic merit, commitment to service, and alignment with our mission to train dedicated healthcare professionals.
