AMNI’s History

If you are passionate about serving humanity through healthcare and wish to study at a medical and nursing institute located in the heart of the city committed to shaping a better tomorrow you’re choosing the right place. At Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute, we don’t rely on formulas or numbers to select students. We carefully review each application academic and personal to welcome students from all walks of life who share our values, compassion, and dedication to making a difference in the medical field. If you would like to pursue a career in nursing, medicine, or healthcare, this is the place where your journey begins.

If you would like to study at a medical and nursing institute located in the heart of the city focused on transforming healthcare for a better tomorrow then Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute is the right choice for you. We don’t rely on rigid formulas to select students. Instead, we carefully review each applicant’s academic record and personal background to find individuals who truly align with our values and can contribute meaningfully to our diverse and compassionate learning community.

Our Philosophy

At Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute, we believe that true healthcare begins with compassion, dedication, and a deep rooted commitment to humanity. Our philosophy is centered around nurturing the next generation of medical and nursing professionals who not only excel in clinical expertise but also carry the values of empathy, ethics, and continuous learning. We strive to create a transformative educational environment where every student feels inspired to serve and uplift communities with care, dignity, and purpose.

Our Core Principle

At Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute, our guiding principle is to cultivate a spirit of integrity, academic excellence, and compassionate service. We are committed to shaping future medical and nursing professionals who lead with knowledge, act with empathy, and uphold the highest ethical standards in healthcare. Through disciplined learning, innovative thinking, and community responsibility, we prepare our students to make a meaningful difference in the world around them.

Key Of Success

At Aman Ullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute, the key to success lies in a harmonious blend of quality education, practical training, and unwavering commitment to ethical healthcare practices. We believe that success is not only measured by academic achievements but also by the ability to serve humanity with compassion, resilience, and integrity. By empowering our students with the right knowledge, values, and clinical skills, we unlock their potential to thrive as future leaders in the medical and nursing fields.

Message From Chairman

All set to become a landmark in the field of health care, Amanullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute, has gone leaps and bounds to deliver its mission of bringing quality healthcare services for all. We are aligned with our guiding principle “Where healthcare transforms”, which is the virtue by which every individual at Amanullah Khan Medical & Nursing Institute works. The team of our expert medical & allied health sciences professionals will constantly endeavour to provide accessible, affordable and best available healthcare education in Pakistan.

Message From CEO

We are trying to provide our students state-of-the-art learning facilities through our purpose-built laboratories and modern research equipments. All our resources are round the clock available to our students. We have developed an interactive learning environment where students can learn and practice to become the leaders of tomorrow in the medical professional field. We aim to train our students to meet the global standards for medical & allied health sciences profession.
