AMNI College of Nursing

Admissions Open for 2025

AMNI College of Nursing is dedicated to providing high-quality nursing education and training. Located in a vibrant academic setting, the college offers a robust curriculum that combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on clinical experience, preparing students for successful careers in healthcare. Our experienced faculty are committed to mentoring and supporting students throughout their educational journey. Equipped with modern facilities and a focus on community engagement, AMNI College of Nursing empowers students to excel in nursing practice and contribute positively to the health and well-being of individuals and communities.

Admission Information

Requirements Application Process Required Documents Fee Structure Scholarships Important Dates

Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

To be eligible for admission to AMNI College of Nursing, candidates must meet the following academic criteria:

  • F.Sc. Pre-Medical with minimum 60% marks
  • Matriculation with Science subjects
  • Age between 17-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • English proficiency test

Personal Requirements

We look for candidates with the following personal qualities:

  • Compassion and empathy
  • Strong communication skills
  • Physical and mental fitness
  • Commitment to healthcare
  • Professional attitude

Additional Requirements

Additional requirements for successful admission:

  • Character certificate
  • Domicile certificate
  • Recent passport size photographs
  • CNIC copy
  • Interview and aptitude test

Application Process

Step 1: Online Application

Begin your journey by accessing our online application portal. AMNI College of Nursing makes it easy for aspiring students to take the first step toward a rewarding healthcare career.

  • Visit our official website
  • Fill out the online application form
  • Upload required documents
  • Pay application fee online

Step 2: Document Submission

Submit all required documents either online or in person at our admission office. Ensure all documents are properly attested and verified.

  • Academic certificates and transcripts
  • Identity documents
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Character certificate

Step 3: Interview & Test

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview and aptitude test. This is an opportunity to express your motivation and passion for healthcare.

  • Personal interview
  • Aptitude test
  • English proficiency test
  • Medical examination

Required Documents

Academic Documents

All academic documents must be properly attested and verified:

  • Matriculation certificate and mark sheet
  • F.Sc. Pre-Medical certificate and mark sheet
  • Character certificate from previous institution
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)
  • Equivalence certificate (if applicable)

Identity Documents

Identity and personal documents required for admission:

  • CNIC copy (student and parents)
  • Domicile certificate
  • Recent passport size photographs (6 copies)
  • Birth certificate
  • Family registration certificate

Medical Documents

Health and medical fitness documents:

  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Blood group certificate
  • Hepatitis B vaccination certificate
  • Eye examination report
  • General health checkup report

Fee Structure

Tuition Fees

Our fee structure is designed to be affordable while maintaining high educational standards:

  • Application Fee: Rs. 2,000
  • Admission Fee: Rs. 25,000
  • Monthly Tuition Fee: Rs. 8,000
  • Laboratory Fee: Rs. 3,000 per semester
  • Examination Fee: Rs. 2,000 per semester

Additional Charges

Other charges that may apply during the program:

  • Library Fee: Rs. 1,000 per semester
  • Sports Fee: Rs. 500 per semester
  • Student Welfare: Rs. 1,000 per semester
  • Uniform Fee: Rs. 5,000 (one time)
  • Clinical Training Fee: Rs. 10,000 per year

Payment Options

We offer flexible payment options to ease financial burden:

  • Full payment at admission
  • Semester-wise payment
  • Monthly installments
  • Online payment facility
  • Bank transfer options

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Merit Scholarships

We offer various scholarships based on academic excellence:

  • 100% scholarship for top 5% students
  • 50% scholarship for top 10% students
  • 25% scholarship for top 20% students
  • Special merit scholarships
  • Academic excellence awards

Need-Based Aid

Financial assistance for deserving students:

  • Income-based fee reduction
  • Orphan student support
  • Disability support programs
  • Rural area student discounts
  • Emergency financial aid

Special Programs

Additional support programs for students:

  • Work-study programs
  • Part-time job opportunities
  • Book and uniform assistance
  • Transportation support
  • Meal assistance programs
