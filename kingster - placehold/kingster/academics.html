BS Nursing

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) program is designed to prepare students for professional nursing practice. This comprehensive program combines theoretical knowledge with practical clinical experience.

  • Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • Credit Hours: 132
  • Clinical Training: 2000+ Hours
  • Accreditation: Pakistan Nursing Council

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work in various healthcare settings including hospitals, clinics, community health centers, and educational institutions.

  • Registered Nurse (RN)
  • Clinical Nurse Specialist
  • Nurse Educator
  • Public Health Nurse
  • Nurse Administrator

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission, candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • F.Sc. Pre-Medical (60% marks)
  • Age: 17-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • English proficiency
  • Interview and aptitude test

Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm-D)

Program Overview

The Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm-D) program is a professional degree that prepares students for careers in pharmaceutical care, drug therapy management, and patient counseling.

  • Duration: 5 Years (10 Semesters)
  • Credit Hours: 180
  • Clinical Rotations: 1500+ Hours
  • Accreditation: Pharmacy Council of Pakistan

Career Opportunities

Pharm-D graduates have diverse career options in pharmaceutical industry, healthcare, research, and academia.

  • Clinical Pharmacist
  • Hospital Pharmacist
  • Pharmaceutical Researcher
  • Drug Safety Specialist
  • Pharmacy Manager

Admission Requirements

Eligibility criteria for Pharm-D program:

  • F.Sc. Pre-Medical (60% marks)
  • Age: 17-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • English proficiency
  • Pharmacy aptitude test

BS Medical Lab Technology

Program Overview

The BS Medical Lab Technology program trains students in laboratory procedures, diagnostic testing, and medical research techniques essential for healthcare diagnosis.

  • Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • Credit Hours: 130
  • Laboratory Training: 1800+ Hours
  • Accreditation: Allied Health Council

Career Opportunities

Medical lab technologists work in diagnostic laboratories, hospitals, research institutions, and public health facilities.

  • Medical Laboratory Technologist
  • Clinical Laboratory Scientist
  • Research Assistant
  • Quality Control Specialist
  • Laboratory Supervisor

Admission Requirements

Requirements for BS Medical Lab Technology:

  • F.Sc. Pre-Medical (55% marks)
  • Age: 17-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Basic computer skills
  • Laboratory aptitude test

BS Anesthesia

Program Overview

The BS Anesthesia program prepares students to assist anesthesiologists in surgical procedures, pain management, and critical care settings.

  • Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • Credit Hours: 135
  • Clinical Training: 2200+ Hours
  • Accreditation: Allied Health Council

Career Opportunities

Anesthesia technologists work in operating rooms, intensive care units, and pain management clinics.

  • Anesthesia Technologist
  • Operating Room Technician
  • Critical Care Assistant
  • Pain Management Technician
  • Emergency Care Specialist

Admission Requirements

Eligibility criteria for BS Anesthesia:

  • F.Sc. Pre-Medical (60% marks)
  • Age: 17-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Physical stamina assessment
  • Technical aptitude test

Pharmacy Technician (Category B)

Program Overview

The Pharmacy Technician program trains students to assist pharmacists in medication dispensing, inventory management, and patient care services.

  • Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)
  • Credit Hours: 70
  • Practical Training: 800+ Hours
  • Accreditation: Pharmacy Council of Pakistan

Career Opportunities

Pharmacy technicians work in retail pharmacies, hospitals, and pharmaceutical companies.

  • Pharmacy Technician
  • Hospital Pharmacy Assistant
  • Retail Pharmacy Staff
  • Pharmaceutical Sales Representative
  • Inventory Manager

Admission Requirements

Requirements for Pharmacy Technician program:

  • Matriculation (Science) with 50% marks
  • Age: 16-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Basic mathematics skills
  • Interview and aptitude test

Diploma in Lady Health Visitor (LHV)

Program Overview

The LHV diploma program prepares female healthcare workers to provide maternal and child health services in community settings.

  • Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)
  • Credit Hours: 65
  • Community Training: 600+ Hours
  • Accreditation: Pakistan Nursing Council

Career Opportunities

LHV graduates work in community health centers, maternal and child health programs, and rural healthcare facilities.

  • Lady Health Visitor
  • Community Health Worker
  • Maternal Health Specialist
  • Child Health Coordinator
  • Family Planning Counselor

Admission Requirements

Eligibility criteria for LHV program:

  • Matriculation with 50% marks
  • Age: 16-30 years (Female only)
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Community service interest
  • Interview and aptitude test

Diploma in Community Midwife (CMW)

Program Overview

The CMW diploma program trains students to provide safe maternal and newborn care services in community and healthcare settings.

  • Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)
  • Credit Hours: 68
  • Clinical Training: 700+ Hours
  • Accreditation: Pakistan Nursing Council

Career Opportunities

CMW graduates work in maternity units, community health centers, and rural healthcare facilities.

  • Community Midwife
  • Maternity Care Assistant
  • Neonatal Care Specialist
  • Maternal Health Educator
  • Family Planning Worker

Admission Requirements

Requirements for CMW program:

  • Matriculation with 50% marks
  • Age: 16-30 years (Female only)
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Interest in maternal care
  • Interview and aptitude test
